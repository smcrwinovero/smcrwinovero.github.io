Seignior Manufacturing Corp.

Aiming to create a reliable, dependable, committed and dedicated organization, the name SEIGNIOR MANUFACTURING CORPORATION was coined and registered in February 2021.

Our Goals of Application

To be a feed mill that produces the highest quality feeds while maintaining the highest level of feed safety in the country

To enhance and tap business opportunities outside the current clientele.

To be always up to date in the current trends, technology and prices in the market.

To support small and medium livestock businesses by providing the best quality in products.

To enrich the lives of the families of its stakeholders.

Our Team

At Seignior Manufacturing Corporation, we believe that only motivated and dedicated staff should, at all times, constitute the company’s work force. We have at least five (5) engineers and a chemical technician to ensure the efficiency and quality of work and produce. We also maintain close liaison with respected consultants in the industry whose opinions are constantly sought regarding new products and technology. The company currently has a work force of twenty-five (25).
